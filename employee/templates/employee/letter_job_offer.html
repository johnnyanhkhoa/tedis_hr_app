We are happy to announce that you have been selected to fill the position of C&B Officer at The Representative Office of TEDIS in Ho Chi Minh City (here in after referred to as “Rep Office”) on the following terms and conditions:

I. Vị trí công việc: Anh/ Chị sẽ đảm nhận vị trí công việc Nhân viên Chế độ chính sách. Tuy nhiên công việc có thể được thay đổi tùy theo yêu cầu tổ chức của VPĐD và phù hợp với công việc đã được thỏa thuận.
Position: You shall be appointed as C&B Officer. The Rep Office reserves the right to after your designation in the event of any re - organization or at any time that the Rep Office deems fit, provided that such alternation is suitable with the agreed work.

II. Thời gian thử việc: Thời gian bắt đầu là ngày 01 tháng 08 năm 2019 đến 30 tháng 09 năm 2019.
Probation period: Your starting date will be started from 1 August 2019 to 30 September 2019.

     Medical Examination: When you start of employment with Rep Office. You will be subjected to a successful completed medical examination conducted by a certified doctor.

Being the position C&B Officer offered to you, we expect you will carry out your duties carefully, with probity and professionalism, guaranteeing also that jobs are accomplished on time regardless the working hour schedule.

Please sign and return the duplicate copy of this letter to the Human Resources Department indicating your acceptance of this offer of employment.
